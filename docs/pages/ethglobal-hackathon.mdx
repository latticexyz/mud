## ETHGlobal Autonomous Worlds Hackathon

On May 18 - 26, Lattice, 0xPARC, and ETHGlobal are co-hosting the [Autonomous Worlds Hackathon](https://ethglobal.com/events/autonomous) to explore the frontiers of onchain games, virtual worlds, and the tooling and scaling that enable them.
